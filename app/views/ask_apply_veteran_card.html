Do you want to apply for a Veteran ID card?

You have completed step one, which confirms that you have served in HM armed forces.

Step 2 involves verifying your identity. This is an extra level of security to help protect you against online identity theft.

You can do this by applying for a Veteran ID card. This is a form of ID that will give you access to services and other benefits you are entitled to as a veteran. You won't need to complete a verification process again.

If you would rather not have a card, but want to prove your veteran status, you still need to prove your identity.
