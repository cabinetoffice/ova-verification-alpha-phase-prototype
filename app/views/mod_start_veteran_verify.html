Apply for a veterans card

Use this service to:

  • Verify that you served in the armed forces
  • Apply for a veteran ID card

Use this service to verify that you have served in HM aremed forces. You can do this fort yourself, or on behalf of another living person if you have their permission

You can use this service if you are (or the person you're requesting information about is):

  • A former member of the armed forces
  • Someone who is authorised to verify a former member of the armed forces

You can also register by post

The online service is also available in Welsh (Cymraeg)

You cannot register for this service if you are in the UK illegally

What you will need:

You will need to create an account and prove your identity before you can verify you are a veteran and apply for a veteran ID card

  • all of your addresses you have lived at for the past 5 years and the dates you lived at them
  • your service number
  • your National Insurance number
  • your passport
  • your driving licence
  • a passport style photo
  • your enlistment and discharge dates
#} {#

Before you start

Do you want to verify veteran status for someone else?

  1. You will need answer some questions about your military service.
    This will take about 5 minutes.
  2. You will then need to verify your identity. The process takes about 15 minutes and is there to protect you from online fraud.

You will need:

  • a passport or driving licence
  • your National Insurance number
  • details about financial matters such as mortgage, loans or other contracts you have
  • a recent passport style photograph of yourself to be used on the card
