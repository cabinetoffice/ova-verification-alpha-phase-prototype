Verify you are a veteran

Use this service to:

  • Confirm that you've served in HM armed forces. This is a basic check with Ministry of Defence to confirm your veteran status.

You will need:

  • your service number
  • your passport or your driving licence

It takes about 5 minutes. You will receive an email within 24 hours. You can use this to access services that require proof of veteran status.

Apply for an ID card

This is a permanent form of ID issued as a physical or digital card. It's valid for accessing all services that veterans are eligible for and other benefits that come from recognition of service. You won't have to go through a verification process to confirm your status again.

You will need:

  • all of your addresses you have lived at for the past 3 years and the dates you lived at them
  • your service number
  • your National Insurance number
  • your passport
  • a passport style photo
  • your driving licence
  • your enlistment and discharge dates

You will also be asked some security questions. This will help protect you from online fraud.

This service is also available:

  • by post
  • in person at your local drop in centre
